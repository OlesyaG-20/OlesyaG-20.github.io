About Olesya

Training that fits your life

I'm Olesya — a personal trainer with over 20 years of experience in London's most prestigious gyms, including KX Gym, Ultimate Performance Mayfair, and Equinox.

I've helped hundreds of women transform their bodies — and more importantly, how they feel every single day. I don't do cookie-cutter programmes. Every woman I work with gets a plan designed around her body, her schedule, and her goals.

I work in Kensington & Chelsea, so sessions fit into your world — my private gym, your home, or Hyde Park just around the corner. No gimmicks. No fads. Just smart, proven training and the support to make it last.
